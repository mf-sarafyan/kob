## Overview

## Description
Lhee are mischievous winged dogs that share features like humanoid hands and snowy-white wings. They come in three types: lesser, common, and greater, each resembling different dog breeds in size and appearance.

## Behavior
These creatures are known for their prankster nature, often acting like regular dogs or mischievous imps. Their behavior includes typical canine antics, like chasing other animals and being drawn to magical objects.

## Combat
In combat, lhee rely on their bites and possess spell-like abilities, including invisibility and various forms of darkness that they can use multiple times daily.

## Society
Lhee have a structured hierarchy where larger individuals dominate the smaller ones. They typically live in packs on small moons or planetoids and can be challenging to train without starting at a young age, although some may serve as ineffective watchdogs.
